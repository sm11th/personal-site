Food in New York City

Posted May 16, 2024

I have started my internship! It has a very reasonable 50-50 in-person/remote balance. I am in Midtown Manhattan, which is a fine 1-1.5 hour commute.

What I remembered today is that:

  1. I have free will
  2. I am going to be in New York 2-3 days a week for the rest of the summer, and
  3. New York has incredible food!

So instead of doing what I normally do (get to Grand Central around 8:30, kind of hang around for a while, get to the office around 8:50-9:00), I went to Madman Espresso and Bagels for breakfast.

I am still trying to be as busy and productive as possible for my internship! But it definitely helps morale to have a trillion food options for lunch right outside my office. So today I started compiling a list of places that I want to go to around the Grand Central area. Here it is so far:

  • $1.50 pizza near Grand Central
  • Sushi & Co, Lexington Ave
  • Ess-A-Bagel

Hey, I never said it was a long list. And I'm trying to be more realistic, because I can only go to places for a quick breakfast before work, during my 30-minute lunch break, or in the brief period between 5pm and when my train to CT leaves.

Email me if you have any good breakfast or lunch recommendations around Midtown Manhattan!
